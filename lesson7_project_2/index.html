Communication Through The Digital World

by Paulina Matheu

Overview

Ways to communicate have expanded throughout the years immensly with the help of technology. Now, most people have access to communicate easily with just a click of a button.

Everyday people around the world use technology to communicate to others whether it be through texting, emailing, or even social media.
Communication between humans developed along with the developments in science and technology which has lead to a universal widespread. In the 1800s, the invention of the telephone paved way for people to talk to eachother through technology. "Come here, I want to see you." was the first successful words spoken on the telephone. Now, people use their cellphones to not just call, but to text, tweet, and chat.

The usage of social networking has also skyrocketed throughout the generation. Sites such as Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter has been the go-to for communicating with friends and family on the internet. Social media users connect with others by following other people that use the platform, liking and sharing their updates, and commenting on their content. As followers increase, the amount of friends one has may increase as well. Thus, the way people communicate is amplyfied with the help of social media.
